About

A-way is a day trip planner, centered on planning around one main location. Through GPS or search, travellers can add their main location for the day and find additional locations to suit their interests and time needs. A-way's goal is to help our users quickly find and filter nearby locations, allowing users to spend less time searching for recommendations and spend more time enjoying their trip. Planning ahead of time is also simple with A-way - enter a starting point, browse and add your activities, and hit save.

Why Choose Us?

A-way is specifically designed with day trip travellers in mind. Our minimalist interface is intuitive to use, and never feels overloaded with buttons, options, or information. A-way curates ten nearby recommendations based on your search input, you’ll never feel overwhelmed by options.

Never feel stressed searching for reviews again. All the information you need about A-way's recommended locations, such as hours, reviews, and images, are available just by tapping the “Details” button.

Design Decisions

Key Interactions

We focused on creating an efficient and intuitive interface by making our affordances more apparent for our users who are on-the-go.

consistent formatting across pages

distinct interaction styles

labels for buttons

Key Paths

Based on our user testing, we improved the overall flow through the app, reduced the interaction options to prevent confusion, and added an onboarding process.

reduced interaction options per step

added progress bar

Affordances

Reducing the interaction options and visual clutter of our interface made the affordances more clear and improved the user’s experience.

onboarding process clarifies interactions

reduced interaction options

Visual Language

Strengthening and clarifying the interface’s options, type, and colours, allowed us to focus on the visual language and impression we wanted the app to give.

reduced colours + visual clutter

Microinteractions

When going through the main interaction path, users have several micro-interactions that enhance their experience.

implemented personalization features

increased feedback for user actions
